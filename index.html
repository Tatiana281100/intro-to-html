About Me

I always loved arts, colors and creativity. Graphic design, painting, drawing – they are my way of expressing myself and let my ideas flow. Music is also a big part of my life, I can’t go a day without it, and if I hear a good song, I just have to dance. I was diagnosed with type 1 diabetes when I was 8, but honestly, it never stopped me from doing anything I wanted. There is too much to discover in this world, and I love learning new things every day, even if sometimes it’s a challenge.

Since I was young, I felt a deep connection with animals and nature. There is something special about leaving technology aside and just enjoying the beauty of the world, whether it’s a forest walk or just watching birds in the park. A good book can do the same, transport me to another world, and I also enjoy spending time with my family. Many people think appearance is everything, but when they get to know me, they realize tattoos don’t define who someone really is.

"The scars I carry are not marks of defeat, but witnesses of my courage. Every step I take forward proves that darkness does not define my story."

-Tatiana Ruggirello
